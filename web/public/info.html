About this page

This website shows trains traveling from a selected monitored station to Aachen Hbf. You can quickly see scheduled departure, real-time departure (including delay), platform, train line, date, end station, and more. Lists are refreshed automatically every 20 seconds.

The “Next departures” list is ordered by scheduled departure time. The “Most delayed today” and “Most delayed last 7 days” lists show trains with the largest delays or cancellations.

Data is provided by the Deutsche Bahn Timetables API. This site is not affiliated with Deutsche Bahn. Accuracy is not guaranteed; always check official sources if needed.
